Init/adoption shall generate a maintained **adoption guide/runbook for an existing team** — what is changing and why, the new gate/review expectations, how the legacy baseline (`VL-013`) is handled, and a staged transition plan — distinct from individual onboarding (`HS-007`), so a whole team aligns on the process change rather than discovering it piecemeal.
