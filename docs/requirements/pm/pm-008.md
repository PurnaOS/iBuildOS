title: "Adoption & migration coverage"

status: draft

priority: must
---

The system shall compute, from the repo, **adoption coverage** (share of code paths, modules, and issues under management — linked, owned, validated — versus outside it) and **migration burndown** (remaining baselined violations, `VL-013`, and un-migrated incumbent items) as a trend over git history, so a lead can track how much of the repo is managed and how fast the gap is closing.
