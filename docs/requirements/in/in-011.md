The system shall support adopting IBuildOS over a **subset of the repo first and expanding** — configurable in-scope paths/areas (building on `KS-004`), the gate enforced strictly inside adopted areas and leniently or off outside them, and per-area ownership/rules added as coverage grows — so a team can roll out subsystem-by-subsystem (intra-repo, per decision D-005).
